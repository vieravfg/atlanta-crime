What is our Project about?

Our project focuses in the analysis of Atlanta's crime data from 2009 to 2019 and 2020 to 2021. We will identify crime patterns or trends based on different locations in Atlanta.

  • General overview of crime in Atlanta
  • Correlations between demographics and crime
  • Forecasting of crime counts in Atlanta

Machine Learning

We used Atlanta crimes data from 2009 to 2019 to create machine learning models to predict crimes count by day. We tested a various different Models, such as AR, MA, ARIMA, ARIMAX, Prophet. After comparing different models, we decided to present ARIMA and Prophet models.
